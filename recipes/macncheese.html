Mac And Cheese

The best quick mac and cheese around!

Have you ever wanted a mac that is easy to make yet also unbelievably flavorful? Well this is it. The creamiest, cheesiest and easiest mac that every mac lover dreams of. Say goodbye to that powder stuff that comes in a box, cause once you have a taste of this mac you'll never go back.

Ingredients

    3 cups whole milk
    1/2 cup unsalted butter
    1/2 cup all purpose flour
    2 teaspoons kosher salt
    1/2 pound dried pasta
    2 cups mac sauce
    1 1/2 cup grated extra-sharp cheddar
    1/2 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese

"Mac Sauce Recipe

    Heat milk over medium heat until bubbling, 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat.
    Heat butter over medium heat in seperate, heavy-bottom pot. Once melted, add flour and whisk constantly until light brown. Remove from heat
    Slowly pour warm milk, about 1 cup at a time, into butter mixture, whisk constantly. It will initially be very thick and thin out over time.
    Once all the milk has been added, set pot back over medium-high heat and whisk constantly for about 2-3 minutes until silky smooth. Use "spoon test" to make sure it's ready.
    add salt and combine and your mac sauce is done!

Mac Recipe

    Cook pasta in salted boiling water until a little less than al dente. Drain, rinse pasta with cold water, and drain again.
    Add Mac Sauce and both cheeses to a large, heavy-bottom pot and cook over medium heat. stir until cheese is melted.
    Slowly add cooked pasta, stir, and continue cooking while stirring continuously until pasta is hot and steaming.
    Spoon pasta into bowls and enjoy!
