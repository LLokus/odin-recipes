Baby Back Ribs

Flavor packed baby back ribs

Tender, juicy, flavorful ribs that just fall off the bone. So good your family will think you ordered them from a restaurant! This recipe couldn't be more simple!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds pork baby back ribs
  • Aluminum Foil
  • cooking spray
  • 1(18 oz) bottle your favorite BBQ sauce

Recipe

  1. Cut ribs into 4 equal portions.
  2. Tear off aluminum foil big enough to enclose each portion of ribs. Grease each piece with cooking spray.
  3. Brush ribs generously with BBQ sauce. Place ribs on pieces of foil, wrap tightly and refrigerate 8 hours,
  4. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F.
  5. Bake ribs wrapped in foil for 2 1/2 hours. Remove ribs from foil and enjoy!
