Quick and Easy Blackened Salmon

Fire up some quick, cheap, and easy salmon! I may have said cheap but don't worry, the flavor is anything but that! With this blend of cajun spices you're bound to have a great dinner!

  • 2 Tablespoons ground paprika
  • 1 Tablespoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 4 Salmon fillets, skinned and bones removed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
