Quick and Easy Blackened Salmon

Fire up some quick, cheap, and easy salmon! I may have said cheap but don't worry, the flavor is anything but that! With this blend of cajun spices you're bound to have a great dinner!

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons ground paprika
  • 1/2 Tablespoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon onion powder
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground white pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 4 Salmon fillets, skinned and bones removed
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Recipe

  1. Mix all dry ingredients together in a small bowl.
  2. Brush salmon fillets on both sides with 1/4 cup butter, and sprinkle spice mixture evenly.
  3. Heat 2 Tablespoons melted butter in large, heavy skillet over high heat. Add salmon and cook until blackened.
  4. Lift fillets, add remaining butter to skillet, and flip fillets into butter.
  5. Cook until other side is blackened and fish flakes easily with fork.
